Welcome to World
Without Form

World Without Form is a community driven project looking to
create a unique musical experience for our members. Our goal is
to incorporate the under appreciated utility of sound into our
many we expansions. Your blockchain companion on this
adventure will be known as ATOM. The ATOM collection will consist
of 5k randomly generated NFTs on the Ethereum blockchain.

Meet ATOM

ATOM's were designed to be the perfect Blockchain companions.
The collection consists of 5k randomly generated pfps made from
over 160 traits. Each ATOM is powered by a Soul Core. This trait
will determine your unique abilities throughout all of our web3
adventures. This ensures that each ATOM will have unique utility
decided by its traits. The core also doubles as your face trait giving your ATOM a unique personality. As an ATOM owner you will gain access to all of our giveaways, airdrops, next gen expansions and more.
